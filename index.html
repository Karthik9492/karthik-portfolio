About Me

Data Engineer with a
manufacturing edge

I'm a Senior Data and MES Engineer with 7+ years of experience designing scalable data systems across pharmaceutical manufacturing, regulated GxP environments, and enterprise analytics. My work sits at the intersection of data engineering, operational intelligence, and cloud infrastructure.

Currently at Eli Lilly and Company, I architect ETL/ELT pipelines processing millions of manufacturing records daily with 99.9% reliability — reducing pipeline latency by 35% and cutting failures by 40% through automated monitoring frameworks. Every pipeline I build is designed for accuracy, traceability, and GxP compliance.

My technical toolkit spans Azure (Data Factory, Synapse Analytics, Databricks, Microsoft Fabric), AWS (S3, Lambda, Glue, Athena, Redshift), Python, Advanced SQL, PySpark, Apache Airflow, and Power BI. I'm equally at home modeling star-schema warehouses, building CI/CD-ready pipelines, or creating executive dashboards that make complex operational data immediately actionable.

Before moving to the United States, I spent 6 years at Dr. Reddy's Laboratories in India, growing from a Manufacturing Executive through MES Associate to a senior Data Analyst — gaining deep hands-on experience with PAS-X MES, SAP ERP, electronic batch records, and GxP compliance at scale across 500+ batch records monthly.

I hold a Master of Science in Business Analytics & AI from the University of Texas at Dallas, and I'm passionate about Industry 4.0, digital transformation in life sciences, and building data infrastructure that bridges the shop floor and the boardroom.
